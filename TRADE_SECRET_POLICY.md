# Trade Secret and Intellectual Property Protection Policy

> **STRICT LEGAL NOTICE:** This document establishes the governance, protection, and legal liability guidelines applicable to all proprietary technology developed within the scope of the **PoWV Protocol**. The cyber-physical infrastructure, source codes, and hardware associated with this ecosystem are not in the public domain.

## 1. Scope of the Trade Secret

All technical infrastructure not explicitly published under Open Source licenses constitutes a **Trade Secret** and the exclusive intellectual property of the protocol's founder and its controlling corporate entities. 

This includes, but is not limited to:
* **Cyber-Physical Architecture:** The integration logic between physical sensors (telemetry, GNSS, computer vision), IoT components, and secure hardware elements.
* **Firmware and Edge Logic:** Signal processing algorithms (e.g., FFT), industrial scale calibration, and *Edge Computing* communication protocols.
* **Source Code and Smart Contracts:** Convergence, validation, and cryptographic chaining logic used to generate the *Proof of World Validation*.
* **Internal Documentation:** Architecture diagrams, bench hardware schematics, and structural *Tokenomics* not yet disclosed to the public.

## 2. Regulatory Framework and Jurisdiction (Brazil)

The protection of the PoWV Protocol's Intellectual Property (IP) is governed under Brazilian jurisdiction, strictly supported by the **Brazilian Industrial Property Law (Law No. 9,279/96)**.

Under **Article 195 of said law**, the crime of unfair competition and criminal offense is committed by anyone who:
* Publishes, exploits, or utilizes, without authorization, confidential knowledge, information, or data usable in industry, commerce, or service provision.
* Gains unauthorized access, through fraud, breach of contract, or breach of trust, to data protected by corporate secrecy.

## 3. Access Control and Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA)

The business secret protection policy within the PoWV Protocol is active and strict:
* **Private Repositories:** Access to closed code and architecture repositories on GitHub is strictly monitored and restricted.
* **Physical Access (Test Bench):** No physical or legal person (including developers, partners, investors, or consultants) is authorized to inspect the hardware test bench or access development terminals without the prior signing of a strict **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)**.
* **IP Assignment Clause:** Any third-party contribution to the ecosystem (code or hardware) is conditional upon the full and irrevocable assignment of patrimonial Intellectual Property rights in favor of the protocol's central governance.

## 4. Legal Liabilities and Penalties

The PoWV Protocol adopts a zero-tolerance policy against Intellectual Property theft, malicious reverse engineering, and the leaking of strategic data. 

Violation of this policy will result in immediate action under the appropriate legal spheres:
* **Criminal Sphere:** Immediate filing of a criminal complaint with competent authorities (such as the Federal Police) for crimes of unfair competition and violation of industrial secrets.
* **Civil Sphere:** Initiation of litigation for full reparation of losses and damages, including lost profits, financial losses in funding rounds (Venture Capital), and digital asset depreciation.
* **Immediate Termination:** Summary termination of any contracts, partnerships, or Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) in force with the infringing party.
